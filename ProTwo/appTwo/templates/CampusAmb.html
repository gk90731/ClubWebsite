Campus Ambassador Program

About The Program

Campus Ambassadors are university students who work closely with Techno-management club TechnoHub to raise technical as well as management profile on campus and introduce it to new and diverse student groups. Best Campus Ambassadors receive recommendation, for marketing and Publicising Work Domains then share their enthusiasm for making a difference through life-changing inter-college experiences. The program offers a prestigious internship-like experience. They know their campus better than anyone

Why Should You Join Campus Ambassador Program?

Certificate of Appreciation

Certificate of appreciation from TechnoHub BIT Durg after the successful completion of the tenure which can be one of the highlights of your resume.

Recommendation for Marketing & Publicizing Work Domains

Best CAs will be recommended as interns in the startups associated with TechnoHub through 36INC. Also, free passes for the events conducted by the club.

Organise Events

A chance to conduct entrepreneurial events in your college in collaboration with TechnoHub BIT, Durg.

Guidance, Network and Contacts

Working with one of the most dedicated teams of BIT, Durg hence acquiring more skills and professionalism. Learn more about technical and management and grow your network through various college clubs related events.

Do You Have a Question?

The club provides an ambiance for students to prepare and to showcase their skills at technical events and entrepreneurship held all across India and Asia in various colleges with the aim to add more feathers in the cap. The ultimate goal of the TechnoHub club is to educate more students and make robotics and entrepreneurship an everyday phenomenon.
